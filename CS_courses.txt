<p align="justify"><strong>CSC116 - Cybersecurity: An Introduction to Security in Cyber Space&#160;</strong>(3 credits)<span>&#160;</span><br/>An introduction to cybersecurity. Recent incidents. <p align="justify"><strong>CSC210 - Computing for Scientists<span>&#160;</span></strong>(3 credits)<span>&#160;</span><br/>Introduction to computing. Computing applications in science. <p align="justify"><strong>CSC220 - Computer Programming II<span>&#160;</span></strong>(4 credits)<span>&#160;</span><br/>Common APIs including list, priority queue, set, and map, and their efficient implementations in an object-oriented language using fundamental data structures. Lecture 3 hours, laboratory 2 hours.<span>&#160;</span><br/><em>Prerequisites: CSC120&#160;or BTE324 or ECE218, MTH108 or MTH140 or MAS110</em></p>
